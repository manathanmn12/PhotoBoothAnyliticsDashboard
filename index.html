QUESTIONS PLANNERS ASK

Everything facilities, procurement, and decision-makers want to know — answered up front.

Is breathing the oxygen safe?
Our oxygen bar is a recreational wellness experience — not a medical treatment, and we make no medical claims. Guests breathe purified oxygen lightly scented with doTERRA aromatherapy through a fresh nosepiece, in relaxed few-minute sessions. OxygenBar360 is run by a former firefighter-EMT, and an EMT-grade first-aid kit is on site at every event.
How do you keep everything sanitary?
Every guest gets a fresh, single-use nosepiece — never shared, and disposed of in plain sight so the next guest sees it’s new. We sanitize ports and surfaces between guests and keep hand sanitizer on the bar. Clean-per-guest hygiene is non-negotiable at every station.
Are you insured? Can you provide a COI?
Yes. OxygenBar360 is fully insured, and we can provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) for your company or venue on request — so we clear your facilities and procurement requirements without slowing the booking down.
What space and power do you need from us?
Very little. The bar is self-contained and the footprint flexes to your space — a lobby corner, conference floor, rooftop, or outdoor tent, indoor or out. We run on standard venue power and bring our own cords, surge protection, and cable-safe taping. Our team handles full setup and teardown around your run-of-show — zero lift for your team.
What’s included — and how does pricing work?
Every activation includes delivery, setup, trained staff, the full aromatherapy menu, and teardown. Pricing starts at $997 for a 2-hour activation, with 4-, 6-, 8-hour and multi-day options. Rates scale in your favor for longer and multi-day events, and 5,000+ guests get a custom proposal. Every quote is locked for 30 days.
How far in advance should we book?
The sooner the better to lock your date, but we can often accommodate shorter timelines. Get an instant quote in 60 seconds and we’ll confirm availability for your date; a deposit holds the booking.
Can you brand or customize the activation?
Absolutely. We tailor the activation to your event and headcount, offer a curated aromatherapy selection, and can add branded signage and a canopy. After the event you get a recap report with attendance, engagement & ROI stats to share with stakeholders.
